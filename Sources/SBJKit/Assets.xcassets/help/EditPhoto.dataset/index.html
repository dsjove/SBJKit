TITLE

This screen lets you crop and mark up a photo.

How to Use

  • Zoom: Pinch with two fingers to zoom in or out on the photo.
  • Pan: Drag with one finger to move around the image.
  • SF_inset.filled.square.dashed Tap the button or double-tap the image to revert the crop.

Actions

  • SF_checkmark.circle Saves all edits to the photo.
  • SF_x.circle Cancel the edit of this photo.
  • SF_pencil.tip Mark up the photo.
