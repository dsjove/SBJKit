TITLE

This screen lets you freely zoom and pan the photo for detailed inspection. Changes to the image are not saved.

How to Use

  • Zoom: Pinch with two fingers to zoom in or out on the photo.
  • Pan: Drag with one finger to move around the image.
  • SF_inset.filled.square.dashed Tap the button or double-tap the image to restore magnification.

Actions

  • SF_square.and.arrow.up Share this photo.
Tip: This screen is designed for flexible, unconstrained photo viewing.
